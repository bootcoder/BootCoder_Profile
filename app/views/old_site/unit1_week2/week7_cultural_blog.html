Stereotype Threat and Me

Harking back to an earlier blog entry, I identified the following values from successful moments in my life.

  • Accomplishment
  • Achievement
  • Adventure
  • Coolness under fire
  • Cooperation
  • Meaningful work
  • Persistence
  • Quality
  • Responsibility
  • Trustworthiness

If I were to pick one value here that stands above the rest in combating stereotype threat it would have to be cooperation. After all cooperation is the polar opposite of stereotype.

The others don't particularly lend themselves to combating stereotype threat, but I feel that they are terrific values none the less. Accomplishment, Achievement, Adventure, Coolness, Meaningful work, Persistence, Quality and Responsibility are all feelings I look forward to cultivating further during DBC.
