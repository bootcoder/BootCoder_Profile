Exercise 2

Coolness under fire.

I think this is one of the finer traits you can have professionally. Life is full of ups and downs, I have found that it is the truest measure of a (wo)man to see them perform when everything that can go wrong does. Lots of people are trustworthy, responsible, persistent, and so forth. Very few however, can be totally zen when the chips are down and the ride gets bumpy. Because, after all, if it was easy everyone would be doing it.
