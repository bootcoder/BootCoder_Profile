BootCoder

Pairing

What was it like to pair with others to solve challenges?

I've go to say the pairing is absolutely terrific.

Seems like at the end of every pairing session people are saying
thanks so much you really knew what you were doing.
And I'm thinking....Me if it weren't for you I'd be totally sunk!

I guess everyone feels that way. So it's great to be able to work together and grow as a unit.

Was something particularly fun or rewarding?

Pairing in general has been very rewarding thus far.
I love working with other people and sharing knowledge.
Also, it's been a big esteem booster to realize that
I'm not as far behind as I feel staring at the screen alone

Was something frustrating and difficult for you?

In regards to pairing... No not really
If I had to e frustrated by one thing I guess it would be having to
copy paste out of stypi to run code through the interpreter.

Feedback

Receiving Feedback

I've only received one piece of feedback, from Greg for my GPS 1.1.
I felt pretty good about it. The feedback was very positive overall.
The only advise was to clean up a few class definitions which I will certainly heed.

Giving Feedback

I enjoy writing feedback for people I have worked with. The hardest part though is
finding things to critique. It seems like everyone is trying very hard and
our collective low level technical bonafides appear to be sufficent, at least so far....
